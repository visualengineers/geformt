<private> <inner> _createListOfTemplatesBasedOnFormalization(atomType, directionType, rotationType, tmpTemplates, useBoundedRotationInvariance)
Get a list of template objects representing the path of a continous atomic gesture. In contrast to method createTemplateFromGestureDefinition it generates templates even if direction or rotation are not defined. In this case a template for every direction and rotation will be created. In case of concatenation of templates all variations are build.
Parameters:
{GeForMT.CONTACT_TYPES|GeForMT.VECTOR_TYPES|GeForMT.SHAPE_TYPES} atomType
The type of the atomic gesture.
{GeForMT.DIRECTION_TYPES} directionType
The dirction type of the gesture.
{GeForMT.ROTATION_TYPES} rotationType
The rotation type of the gesture.
{Array} tmpTemplates
List of template objects representing variations of the previous part of the gesture. The parts will be connected to a complex gesture path.
